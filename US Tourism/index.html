    The World War II Memorial is a national memorial in the United States
    Dedicated to Americans who served in the armed forces and as civilians during World War II.
    It is located on the National Mall in Washington, D.C.

    The Vietnam Veterans Memorial, commonly called the Vietnam Memorial, is a U.S.
    National memorial in Washington, D.C., honoring service members of the U.S.
    Armed forces who served in the Vietnam War
